What We Have

Lab Equipment

Biosafety Cabinet

Class II laminar flow hood for sterile cell culture and microbiology work.

PCR Thermocycler

For DNA amplification, genotyping, and molecular cloning protocols.

Gel Electrophoresis

Agarose and PAGE systems for DNA, RNA, and protein analysis.

Centrifuges

Microcentrifuges and larger benchtop units for cell and sample prep.

Microscopy

Optical and fluorescence microscopes for imaging cells and organisms.

Fermentation & Incubation

Incubators, shakers, and fermenters for growing cells and cultures.

Chemical Storage

Flammable-rated Justrite cabinets and proper chemical management systems.

Pipetting & Liquid Handling

Full range of Eppendorf and Pipetman pipettes, tips, and consumables.
